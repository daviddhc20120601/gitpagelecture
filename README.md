1. Course Introduction

   Welcome to "MakeYourWebsiteWithGPT," a three-lecture course designed to help kids learn how to create their own websites using GitPages. In this course, students will use text and pictures to build engaging web pages.

2. Teacher Introduction

   In this course, you'll be guided by our experienced teacher, Mr. David Chen. David has a passion for teaching and technology, making learning a fun and engaging experience. With his expertise, you're in good hands for your web development journey.

3. Course Material

   * Lecture 1: 
   Discover what to expect in this course: Course Preview
   Learn the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Markdown.
   Utilize tools like Notepad++ and Git.
   Get acquainted with Markdown.
   Understand the fundamentals of GitPage.
   Practice by forking from this example.
   Homework: Think about the content you want on your website and fill in the provided form.
   * Lecture 2: 
   Explore different types of websites: text-based, picture-based, and AI-powered.
   Prepare a demo for Lecture 3.
   * Lecture 3:
   Join us for an exhibition and demo session.
   Add your work to the gallery.

5. Course Objective

   The main objective of this course is to empower students to create their own websites using GitPages. By learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Markdown, and understanding GitPage basics, students will be well-equipped to express their creativity on the web.

5. Evaluation Method

   Student performance will be assessed based on their completion of homework assignments and active participation in the exhibition and demo session during Lecture 3. The focus will be on creativity and understanding the concepts taught throughout the course.
